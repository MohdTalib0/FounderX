Three levels of risk.

Safe
When you're tired

We launched with 0 followers and 0 email subscribers. No Product Hunt. No Twitter hype. Just a landing page and a cold email to 12 people we respected. 3 of them replied. That was enough to start.

Bold
When you're confident

Stop waiting for an audience before you launch. We had zero. Literally zero. We emailed 12 strangers and 3 said yes. That's a 25% conversion rate. Most Series A companies would kill for that.

Spicy
When you have something to prove

The "build an audience first" advice is a trap. It's procrastination disguised as strategy. We launched to 0 people. Got 3 users from cold email. Those 3 shaped the entire product.
